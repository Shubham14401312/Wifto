Amer Fort

Amer Fort, located in Jaipur, Rajasthan, is a magnificent example of Rajput architecture. Built with red sandstone and marble, the fort overlooks Maota Lake and combines both Hindu and Mughal architectural styles. It is famous for its large ramparts, series of gates, courtyards, and the Sheesh Mahal (Mirror Palace). Amer Fort is a UNESCO World Heritage Site and a major tourist attraction.
