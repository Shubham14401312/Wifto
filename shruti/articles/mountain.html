Mountains of India

India is home to some of the world's most majestic mountain ranges. The Himalayas in the north, including Kanchenjunga, Nanda Devi, and others, form a natural barrier and influence the climate, rivers, and biodiversity. The Vindhyas, Aravalli, and Western Ghats contribute to regional geography, culture, and traditions, making mountains an essential part of India's environment.
