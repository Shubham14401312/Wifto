Thar Desert

The Thar Desert, also called the Great Indian Desert, is located in the northwestern part of India. It spans across Rajasthan and extends into parts of Punjab, Haryana, and Gujarat. The desert is characterized by sand dunes, arid climate, and unique flora and fauna adapted to extreme conditions. Traditional life in the Thar includes camel rides, folk music, and festivals, reflecting the rich cultural heritage of the region.
